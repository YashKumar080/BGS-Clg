Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real payment system?

No. This is a demo simulation. No payment is required or charged. Plan upgrades are instant and free for demonstration purposes.

Will my plan persist after reload?

Yes! Your selected plan is saved in the local SQLite database and will be remembered even after you close and reopen the app.

What limits does the Free plan have?

The Free plan simulates a limit of 5 AI scans per day and up to 5 medicine reminders. Pro and Family plans have no such limits.

How do SOS alerts work?

When a medicine is missed, the system logs an alert and can send email notifications to registered SOS contacts (requires SMTP setup in .env).
